About

Vanguard was founded on Frostwolf at the onset of The Burning Crusade, a Horde raiding guild that conquered every tier of content the expansion had to offer. After more than a decade of silence, it has been restored by one of its founding officers. As of Midnight, Vanguard is rebuilding: a social and leveling home with room to grow, or for some, a sealed artifact from a bygone era.

Guild Rules

Conduct

  • Treat guildmates with respect. We're here to have fun, not to be the fun police — but harassment, slurs, and sustained negativity aren't welcome.
  • Drama stays out of guild chat. Handle personal conflicts privately.

Activity

  • No hard attendance requirements. Real life comes first.
  • If you're going inactive for a while, give us a heads up so we don't remove you by accident.

Grouping

  • You're never obligated to join a group, but if you sign up, show up.
  • Help when you can. Nobody's keeping score, but a guild is a community.

FAQ

How do I join?
Whisper any online officer in-game, or reach out through our Discord. We don't have an application process — just say hello.
Is there a required activity level?
No. Play as much or as little as you want. We only ask that you give us a heads up if you're going to be away for an extended period.
I used to be in Vanguard years ago. Do I have to start over?
No. Returning members are recognized with the Evocatus rank. Just let an officer know who you are.
Alliance or Horde?
Both. Vanguard is cross-faction. The guild has Horde roots but you're welcome regardless of what you play.
